The Decorator pattern is a better choice compared to the composite pattern. The decorator pattern is more suitable in its ability to dynamically add new functionalities such as varied abilities or characterisitics like health and damage resistance to zombies without modifying thier existing code structure. With this approach it allows for a flexible enhancement of individual zombie features, aligning perfecetly with the need for diverse zombie types with unique behaviors. Overall, the decorator pattern is the preferred choice due to its dynamic flexibility and capability.
